Advocate Reward Paid Email¶

Let Advocates know they have been rewarded, or a partial refund has been issued.
The email is triggered and sent when an Advocate receives a reward. It can be a Signup, Share, or Referral reward (when Referral is marked as Approved). All trigger conditions are configured via Incentive Criteria.

Advocate Reward Paid Email template could be customised via Editor.

Frequently used Variables:

  • To show Reward Coupon Code use {{ coupon_code }}.

  • Main CTA should point to a merchant site to start shopping {{ proceed_to_advocate_destination_url }}.

Note

Ideal email width is 480px. This is a most universal width for both mobile and desktop platforms. Read more about email design.

Email sending conditions

By default is sent immediately after the reward status turns into Paid, but can be delayed by configuration.

When the coupon list for Sharing or Signup incentive is empty at the moment of reward issuing, Reward Paid Email will not be sent. We will try to reward the Advocate and send the email in the background for 60 days (with an increasing duration between retries) in the case of coupon list fulfillment.
