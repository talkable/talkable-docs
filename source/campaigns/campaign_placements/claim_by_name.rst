Claim by Name Placement
=======================

By default, campaign will be shown only on /checkout page but it might be changed as you want.
This type of placement used for campaigns that allows customers to fill in advocate username and get a reward.

:ref:`Learn more about Placements <campaigns/campaign_placements>`

Integration instructions
------------------------

Claim by Name Placement requires integration version above `5.1.0`.
Step by step instructions, how to integrate campaign on your website can be found on
:ref:`Integration Claim by Name<integration/claim_by_name>` page.
